Title: List of Common Objections | Welcome To Delhi High Court

205\. MEMO OF PARTIES BE FILED AND SIGNED. COMPLETE ADDRESS BE GIVEN IN MEMO OF PARTY.
206\. Details of impugned order of District Court/Tribunal should be mentioned in Memo of parties.
208\. Order against which petition/application is filed be mentioned in prayer clause and heading.
209\. PETITION/APPLICATIONS/ANNEXURES/ORDER/POWER OF ATTORNEY SHOULD BE STAMPED / COURT FEES SHORT OR MISSING.
210\. PETITION/APPLICATIONS/MOP/INDEX/POWER OF ATTORNEY BE SIGNED AND DATED BY PETITIONERS AND ADVOCATE.
211\. LEFT SIDE MARGIN OF FOUR C.M. BE MAINTAINED / ONLY LEGAL SIZE PAPERS ALLOWED.
212\. Affidavit be filed in support of petition/appeal/application and attested/identified with age/address details.
213\. Name/address/telephone details of counsel/litigants be mentioned in index/memo/vakalatnama.
214\. Rest of objections will be raised later according to correct classification/nomenclature.
215\. Fair typed copy of dim/illegible and handwritten annexures be filed.
216\. Documents be made true copy at bottom of each page and signed.
217\. Certified copy of annexures/orders be filed.
218\. Underline and highlighting be deleted.
219\. PAGE NUMBERING be done in continuation; no alphabetical/alphanumeric/double pagination.
220\. Corrections in cause title/index/memo of parties are not allowed.
221\. English translation of vernaculars be filed.
222\. Proper prayer clause/heading be made.
223\. Correct provision of law be given in heading of appeal/petition/application.
224\. Tribunal should not be made a party.
225\. Annexures be filed on foolscape and one side of paper.
226\. One more set be filed duly signed along with listing proforma.
227\. Petition/application be properly classified and maintainability should be shown.
228\. Mention if similar/connected matter is filed or pending in this Court.
229\. Order/Judgment be filed with name of Court/Judge.
230\. Application for condonation of delay in filing/refiling be filed with affidavit.
231\. BLANKS BE FILLED / TYPED INDEX BE FILED.
232\. Where several persons sign single vakalatnama, signatures should correspond to memo serial numbers.
233\. Advocate/litigants should mention name, address, enrollment number, mobile/phone in index.
234\. Petition/application/appeal be properly filed for scrutiny.
235\. Number of days be given in prayer of delay application.
236\. Brief description of annexures be given in main index.
237\. VAKALATNAMA be filed, dated, signed, welfare stamp affixed, client signature identified.
239\. Valuation of court fee affixed/e-court fee should be indicated in index.
240\. Thumb impression be identified with name and LTI/RTI.
242\. In Memo of Parties, serial numbers should be running numbers; sub-numbering alphabets not allowed.
243\. Petition/application through authorised signatory should have authorisation letter.
244\. Clear copies be filed with proper left side margin.
245\. Petition/Suit/Application be split into volumes with max 200 pages and separate index.
246\. If service through courier/postal means, undertaking of correct address service should be given.
247\. AFFIDAVIT OF CLERK/COUNSEL NOT ALLOWED.
248\. Petition/index/application/document should be typed in double space with font size 14.
249\. Impugned order/application(s) be tagged after main petition/application/appeal.
250\. Application for stay be filed separately with affidavit.
251\. Separate petition/appeal be filed against each case.
252\. Memo of party should list email identities of every party.
253\. One-time PF to be filed by plaintiff at filing and by defendant at WS stage.
254\. Plaint/Suit/Petition be typed on A-4 size white paper.
255\. Plaint/Suit/Petition be fairly and legibly typed.
256\. Plaint/Suit/Petition be typed in proper spacing with 14 font size.
257\. Proper margins be maintained for filing/e-filing.
